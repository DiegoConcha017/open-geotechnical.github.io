PTIM: Boring/Drilling Progress by Time¶

  • Class: Samples, Amalgamated Specimens and Laboratory Test Scheduling
Heading Unit Type Example Description
LOCA_ID   ID 327-16A Location identifier
PTIM_DTIM yyyy-mm-ddThh:mm DT 1991-03-01T14:35 Date and time of progress reading
PTIM_DPTH m 2DP 22.13 Hole depth
PTIM_CAS m 2DP 20.5 Depth of casing
PTIM_WAT m XN 16.56 or Dry Depth to water
PTIM_REM   X Stopped drilling on client’s instruction Remarks
FILE_FSET   X FS21 Associated file reference (eg drilling journals)

Notes - 1¶

  • PTIM is used to record information on the development of the exploratory hole. Readings of depth of hole, casing and water level are required at the start and end of shift, as a minimum by BS 5930:1999.
