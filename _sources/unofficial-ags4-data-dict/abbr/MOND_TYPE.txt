MOND_TYPE
===========================================================================

    * - Code
      - Description
    * - ANGA
      - Rotation/Tilt in direction A
    * - ANGB
      - Rotation/Tilt in direction B
    * - ANGC
      - Rotation in direction C
    * - BAR
      - Barometric pressure at time of monitoring
    * - BRG
      - Bearing
    * - CURR
      - Current
    * - DSPA
      - Displacement in direction A
    * - DSPB
      - Displacement in direction B
    * - DSPC
      - Displacement in direction C
    * - DSTA
      - Distance A from LOCA_ID (slip indicator top rod)
    * - DSTB
      - Distance B from LOCA_ID (slip indicator top rod)
    * - DSTL
      - Distance
    * - EAST
      - Absolute position (Easting)
    * - ERAT
      - Volume extraction rate
    * - FLOW
      - Flow
    * - FORC
      - Force
    * - GAUG
      - Gauge length
    * - GCD
      - Carbon dioxide concentration
    * - GCDP
      - Peak carbon dioxide concentration
    * - GCDS
      - Steady carbon dioxide concentration
    * - GCM
      - Carbon monoxide concentration
    * - GCMP
      - Peak carbon monoxide concentration
    * - GCMS
      - Steady carbon monoxide concentration
    * - GFLO
      - Gas flow rate
    * - GM
      - Measured methane as percentage of LEL
    * - GMP
      - Peak methane as percentage of LEL
    * - GMS
      - Steady methane as percentage of LEL
    * - GOX
      - Oxygen concentration
    * - GOXP
      - Steady oxygen concentration
    * - GOXS
      - Peak oxygen concentration
    * - GPRS
      - Differential Pressure
    * - HYS
      - Hydrogen sulphide concentration
    * - HYSP
      - Peak hydrogen sulphide concentration
    * - HYSS
      - Steady hydrogen sulphide concentration
    * - LEV
      - Absolute position (Level)
    * - NRTH
      - Absolute position (Northing)
    * - PRES
      - Pressuremeter test hole
    * - RAIN
      - Rainfall
    * - RFLOW
      - River flowrate measurement
    * - RLEVEL
      - River level measurement
    * - ROTS
      - Rotational speed
    * - STAT
      - Status
    * - STRA
      - Strain in direction A
    * - STRB
      - Strain in direction C
    * - STRC
      - Strain in direction A
    * - TEMP
      - Atmospheric temperature
    * - TGM
      - Total methane concentration
    * - TGMP
      - Peak total methane concentration
    * - TGMS
      - Steady total methane concentration
    * - TORQ
      - Torque
    * - TRST
      - Thrust
    * - UPT
      - Up/run time
    * - VEL
      - Velocity
    * - VOL
      - Volume
    * - VOLT
      - Voltage
    * - WDEP
      - Depth to water from LOCA_ID datum
    * - WHD
      - Head of water above tip
    * - LNAPL
      - Light Nonaqueous Phase Liquid
    * - DNAPL
      - Dense Nonaqueous Phase Liquid
    * - GNIT
      - Nitrogen concentration
    * - GNITP
      - Peak nitrogen concentration
    * - GNITS
      - Steady nitrogen concentration
    * - HYC
      - Hydrogen cyanide concentration
    * - HYCP
      - Peak hydrogen cyanide concentration
    * - HYCS
      - Steady hydrogen cyanide concentration
    * - GFLOP
      - Peak gas flow rate
    * - GFLOS
      - Steady gas flow rate
    * - DOWNTEMP
      - Downhole temperature
