Notes - 2
-------------------------
- FRAC is used to include fracture set details derived from rock core. Fracture information collected from scanline surveys of rock exposures or similar should be recorded in the DISC group.
- It is not typical to define fracture set spacing?s as both min/avg/max fracture spacing and Fracture Index. The requirement for one or other should be specified.
